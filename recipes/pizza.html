My Favorite Vegan Pizza

Description of Recipe

This recipe is here to make a tasty vegan pizza! Even if you're dairy free, you don't have to give up one of your favorite meals. Follow the instructions to get your pizza night off to a tasty start!

Ingredients

  • Trader Joe's pizza crust
  • 1/2 cup each of red, orange, and green bell pepper
  • 1/3 cup red onion
  • 1 cup mushrooms
  • 1/2 teaspoon each garlic, basil, and garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (218 C) and position a rack in the middle of the oven.
  2. Bring large skillet to medium heat. Once hot, add 1 Tbsp olive oil (amount as original recipe is written // adjust if altering batch size), onion and peppers.
